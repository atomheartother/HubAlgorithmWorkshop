===
THE SPEECH
===

Pedro is now running for president in the United States. He managed to get a fake birth certificate, and despite not speaking a word of English, he is now in the Presidential race!
But today he has to give a big speech in a swing state. This speech could make or break his whole campaign - and Pedro doesn't speak a word of english! He will have to be as brief as possible, but very effective!

Requirements:

- Recursion
- Bitwise operators

===
EXERCISE
===

Make a speech() function prototyped like this:

int     speech(uint64_t n);

speech() should return the number of bits set to 1 in the int passed as parameter.

You are only allowed one instruction, so one ';' character.

Forbidden keywords:
- while
- for
- goto
- malloc
- asm
